Robotics Graduate Student

I am a second-year Robotics masters student in Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I completed my undergraduation from Savitribai Phule Pune University (formerly Pune University) majoring in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. My research interest lies in the areas of perception, deep learning 3D computer vision, robot localization and mapping problems.

I am currently working on Sensor-Fusion of IMU and stereo camera (Visual Inertial Odometry). I have experience in Camera Calibration, Multi-view geometry, and Visual SLAM. I am actively looking for Summer Internships in 2023 in Deep Learning and Computer Vision.

During my masters, I have explored Robot Navigation with Prof. Daniel Montrallo Flickinger, Robot Controls with Prof. Siavash Farzan and it's Perception with Prof. Nitin J Sanket. My recent research at WPI in Deep Learning includes implementation of Learning to Rank Models (L2R) to find the encrypted subkeys on AES.

Currently, I am working at DigiM LLC as an Image Analysis Intern. My responsibilites includes implementing machine learning, deep learning and 3D segmentation solutions on 3D microscopic images to perform quantitative analysis, reconstructing 3D materials achieving predictive property simulation.

In my spare time I like to swim, play soccer, go on hikes and watch animes!
